# Support

Need help with Next Services, want to report a bug, or have a feature suggestion?

**Email us at: [NextServicesUK@gmail.com](mailto:NextServicesUK@gmail.com)**

We aim to respond within 3-5 business days.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is my location shared?
No. Your location is used only on your device to show your position on the map and find services nearest to you. It's never sent to our servers or shared with third parties.

### How do you make money?
The app is free with banner ads provided by Google AdMob. A future update may introduce a premium subscription that removes ads.

### Can I suggest a missing service area or amenity?
Yes, please. Email us with details and we'll add it in the next data update.

### How do you handle data for users in the UK and EU?
We comply with UK GDPR and EU GDPR. See our [Privacy Policy](./privacy.html) for details.
